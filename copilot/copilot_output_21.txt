**Purpose**

This repository is a small Hoare-logic / program-verification project, structured as a student assignment. The intent is to represent a tiny imperative language as Python ASTs, compute weakest preconditions and verification conditions over that language, and optionally discharge those conditions with Z3.

The key pieces are:
- Expression ASTs in Exprs.py for arithmetic and boolean terms such as AEVar, AEVal, AEPlus, BELt, and BEAnd.
- Imperative-command ASTs in Imp.py for Skip, Assgn, Seq, IfThen, and While.
- Logical specification ASTs plus substitution helpers in Specs.py.
- The unfinished assignment targets in WPrec.py and VCs.py: wprec(c, q), VC(pre, c, post), and VCG(pre, c, post).
- Optional theorem-prover integration in Z3Driver.py, which translates specs to Z3 and checks the generated obligations.
- Unit tests in test_Specs.py, test_WPrec.py, test_VCs.py, and test_Z3Driver.py.

In its current state, WPrec.py and VCs.py still contain pass statements, so this checkout is a scaffold rather than a finished verifier.
